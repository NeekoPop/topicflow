ℹ️ About TopicFlow

What is TopicFlow?

TopicFlow is an AI-powered educational assistant designed to help students transform their study materials into structured learning resources. Built for the AI Midterm Exam 2026, this application leverages advanced AI technology to generate summaries, quizzes, and flashcards from any study material.

Features

  • 📋 AI Summarizer: Generate concise, bulleted summaries of your study materials
  • ❓ Quiz Generator: Create interactive multiple-choice quizzes with explanations
  • 🎴 Flashcard Maker: Extract key terms and definitions for memorization

How to Use

  1. Enter your study material in the Material Input tab
  2. Choose which feature you want to use (Summary, Quiz, or Flashcards)
  3. Click the corresponding button to generate AI-powered content
  4. Review and interact with the generated content
